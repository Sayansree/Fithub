What is FitHub?

It is an web-app that is quite literally for the people and by the people. Inspired from GitHub contribution chart, we have made this project "FitHub". Just like the green squares inspires us code and contribute to open source, similarly the blue squares of FitHub inspires you to be fit and stay committed to excersing daily. Not only that, to encourage people more, we also have an idea to collaborate with NGO so that when a person completes 1000 commits, a food packet can be donated to needy people un the name of the person. This way we can fight against hunger and remain fit as well.

Learn More

Our Aim

It is estimated that 11000 people die from obesity-related diseases every day around the world, while the number of deaths worldwide for hunger or malnutrition illness is about 24000 per day. While some people are spending millions just to stay fit, some even do not have a penny so that they can stay fit. Now what if we can kill two birds with one stone, i.e., what if we can fight two of the world's greatest problems- obesity and hunger with just one solution? All that is needed is a little motivation and kindness. Therefore, our team has come up with "Fithub", providing fitness to all.

Frequently Asked Questions

Know more about us

  • What is our project "FitHub"?

    Fithub is a web app that is quite literally for the people and by the people. It is a user-friendly platform in which you can put your daily workout goals or just any workout you have done in a day as commits. Similar to that of Github, Fithub too has a contribution calendar. The only difference is that, in Fithub, The contribution blocks, or as we call it Fit-Commits are in blue, and the more the number of commits, the darker will the blocks get. But the catch here is that, if a person reaches a mark of 1000 commits, then an NGO tied up with our organization will provide food packets to two people in the name of the person reaching the mark.

  • How it will help you?

    FitHub will help you keep track of your fitness journey through a day, month, and years to come.

  • How beneficial is FitHub for society?

    There are a lot of fitness freaks, who just spend hours in the gym doing different kinds of exercise. According to us, it will be highly beneficial for the poor as such persons will be able to a lot of fit commits per day.

  • What are our thoughts?

    We believe our web app will also serve as a medium of motivation for people wanting to work out but can't find themselves sticking to it as their little effort will bring happiness to someone else.

  • What's next for "FitHub"?

    We are planning to put our web app to real-time use, as we believe this is something that the world needs right now, thus benefitting a lot of different kind of people.
